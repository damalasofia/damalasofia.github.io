about

Sofia Damala is an Athens-based artist working across painting, mixed media, installation, video and digital arts.

Holder of an MA in Digital Arts, her practice explores the intersection of physical and digital space — combining conceptual research with sensory and emotional experience. Her work investigates the essence of form and synthesis, moving fluidly between traditional and digital media.
