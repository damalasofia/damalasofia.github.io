About

Fine Artist
based in Athens

Sofia Damala is an Athens-based contemporary artist working across painting, mixed media, installation, video and digital arts. Holder of an MA in Digital Arts, her practice explores the intersection of physical and digital space — combining conceptual research with sensory and emotional experience.

Her work investigates the essence of form and synthesis, moving fluidly between traditional and digital media to examine how space, concept and materiality converge.
